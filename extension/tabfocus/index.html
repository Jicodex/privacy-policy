Privacy Policy - TabFocus Video Control

Last updated: 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how our browser extension operates and how we handle user data. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from users. The extension operates locally within your browser.

2. How the Extension Works

The extension automatically pauses videos when you switch tabs and resumes playback when you return. This functionality is performed entirely within your browser and does not involve any external servers.

3. Permissions Usage

The extension may request browser permissions such as tabs and storage only to provide its core functionality. These permissions are used strictly for controlling video playback and saving user preferences.

4. Data Sharing

5. Security

Since all functionality runs locally in your browser, your data remains secure and under your control.

6. Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party analytics, tracking tools, or external services.
